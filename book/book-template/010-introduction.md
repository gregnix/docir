# Introduction

Replace this with your opening chapter. Headings carry **no** numbers here;
`book-build.tcl -number` adds them hierarchically (1, 1.1, 1.1.1 ...).

Mark index terms inline with a bracketed span: a [keyword]{.index} stays in
the running text and is also collected into the subject index. Multi-word
terms work too — a [build pipeline]{.index} becomes one index entry.

## A section

Sections become sub-numbers (1.1, 1.2 ...) and appear in the table of
contents. Anchors are derived from the title, so cross-references stay
valid even if you reorder chapters later.
